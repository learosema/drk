### Leading Question

What makes the GRC more than an ambulance service? It connects everyday
assistance and crisis response in a single system — from nurseries to civil
protection, from emergency call buttons to international aid.

### Tracing Service & International Responsibility

The GRC tracing service reunited 16 million people after World War II. Today:
40,000 family tracing consultations per year. In 2024, over 3.1 million people
affected by crises abroad were reached.

> "The GRC is not a crisis responder that only appears when cameras are rolling.
> It is a permanent social infrastructure system — every day, everywhere in
> Germany."
