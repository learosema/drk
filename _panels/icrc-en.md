### Leading Question

Who acts in war when everyone else falls silent? The ICRC — operating in armed
conflicts since 1863.

### Mandate & Legal Basis

The ICRC operates under the mandate of the 1949 Geneva Conventions and their
Additional Protocols. It is neutral, impartial and independent. Its mandate: to
protect and assist people affected by armed conflict and violence — and to
promote respect for international humanitarian law (IHL).
