### Situation

Since 24 February 2022, Ukraine has been the site of one of the largest armed
conflicts in Europe since the Second World War. Destroyed infrastructure, winter
temperatures down to –20 °C, power outages and forced displacement define daily
life for millions. In 2025, 12.7 million people need humanitarian aid.

### What GRC and ICRC are doing

| Programme                                    | Region                             | Budget |
| -------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------ |
